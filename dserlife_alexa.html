How do I connect to Alexa?

 

1. Complete product networking configuration in the dserlife App

Complete the device's networking configuration according to the prompts in the App

Note: In the App, change the name of the device to an easily identifiable name such as Alexa; names are usually in English, such as "Robot "

2. Configure the Amazon Echo device

If you have already configured Amazon Echo, you can skip this step. The following instructions are based on the Andiord client

a. Make sure your Amazon Echo device is powered on and connected to a Wi-Fi network.

b. Open the Alexa app on your phone. After successful login, tap "+" on the menu in the upper right corner of the Home page, tap "Add Device" to set up the Amazon Echo.

c. Choose your Amazon Echo device for connecting. Press and hold the small dot on the device until the light turns orange. While your device’s name appears on app page, tap it.Then select your Wi-Fi network, Amazon Echo will take a few minutes to try to connect to the network. After connecting to the Amazon Echo hotspot, the connection is successful. You can tap "Continue" to proceed to the next step or not.

3. Key step - link Skill

a. Return to the menu page, tap "≡" in the upper left corner of the Home page, then tap on "Skills & Games" in the Alexa app menu.

b. Then search for "smartlife". Tap "Enable" to enable the Skill.

c. Enter the dserlife APP account and password, then tap “Link Now” to link your dserlife account to enable the Skill. Now you can start your smart home journey.

Common commands

Control the device via voice commands Now you can control your smart device with Echo. You can control your device (such as Robot) with the following commands:

Alexa, turn on Robot.

Alexa, turn off Robot.
